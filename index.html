Our Roots

Born in Sour —
the City That Sailed the World

Sour (Tyre) was the greatest maritime city of the ancient world. The Phoenicians who sailed from this very shore were the first true global explorers — trading, connecting, and discovering across the Mediterranean and beyond.

Ocean Over Seas was founded with that same spirit: a deep Lebanese passion for the world, rooted in the city that first dared to cross the horizon. When you travel with us, you travel with 3,000 years of seafaring heritage behind you.

Deeply local, globally connected
Personal service — we know your name, not just your booking number
